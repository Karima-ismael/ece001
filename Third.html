Artificial Intelligence In Robots

How should this knowledge be represented? How should this knowledge be used? Robots challenge artificial intelligence by forcing them to deal with real things in the real world. The techniques and representations developed for pure cognitive problems, often in game areas, do not necessarily extend to meeting the challenge.
